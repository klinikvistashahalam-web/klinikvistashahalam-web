# How to Make Your Website Findable on Google Search

## Prerequisites
✓ Website published on GitHub Pages
✓ URL: https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/clinic-website/

---

## Step 1: Submit to Google Search Console

1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Click **"Start now"** and sign in with your Google account
3. Click **"Add property"**
4. Enter your website URL: `https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/clinic-website/`
5. Click **"Continue"**

---

## Step 2: Verify Ownership

Google will provide you with a verification HTML file:

1. Download the verification file (e.g., `google1234567.html`)
2. Go to your GitHub repository: https://github.com/YOUR-USERNAME/clinic-website
3. Click **"Add file"** → **"Upload files"**
4. Drag and drop the verification file
5. Click **"Commit changes"**
6. Wait 1 minute for GitHub to deploy
7. Go back to Google Search Console
8. Click **"Verify"**

---

## Step 3: Submit Your Sitemap

1. In Google Search Console sidebar, click **"Sitemaps"**
2. In the text field, enter: `sitemap.xml`
3. Click **"Submit"**
4. You should see "Success" status

---

## Step 4: Request Indexing

1. In Google Search Console sidebar, click **"URL Inspection"**
2. Enter your homepage URL: `https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/clinic-website/`
3. Wait for inspection to complete
4. Click **"Request Indexing"**
5. Confirm the request

---

## How to Check If You're Indexed

Method 1: Use Google Search Console
- Go to Coverage report to see indexed pages

Method 2: Manual Search
- Go to Google.com
- Search: `site:YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/clinic-website`
- You'll see all indexed pages from your site

## Troubleshooting

**Problem**: Verification fails
- **Solution**: Wait 2-3 minutes after uploading verification file, then try again

**Problem**: Sitemap not found
- **Solution**: Check that sitemap.xml is in the root directory and accessible at:
  `https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/clinic-website/sitemap.xml`

**Problem**: Pages not appearing in search
- **Solution**: Be patient. It can take 2-4 weeks. Check robots.txt isn't blocking crawlers.

**Problem**: "URL is not on Google"
- **Solution**: This is normal for new sites. Submit for indexing and wait.
